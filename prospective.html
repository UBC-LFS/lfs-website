Founded in 1914, the UBC Faculty of Land and Food Systems has worked for over a century to address global issues surrounding health and sustainable land and food systems. Today, our world faces unprecedented challenges of climate change, population growth, food and nutrition insecurity, and water shortages, challenges that our researchers and students are hard at work to solve. As one of the top 25 Life Sciences Schools in the world, the Faculty is uniquely positioned to educate and empower the next generation of problem solvers.
