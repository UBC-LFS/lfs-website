Aboriginal Students

The Faculty of Land and Food Systems is Honored to exist on the traditional, ancestral, and unceded territory of the hən̓q̓əmin̓əm̓ speaking Musqueam people. In this acknowledgement we as a Faculty recognize the need to build strong community relationships with Indigenous people and to provide unique opportunities for Aboriginal students on campus.
