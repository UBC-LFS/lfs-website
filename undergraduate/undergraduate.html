UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

At UBC's Faculty of Land and Food Systems (LFS), we take an interdisciplinary approach to investigating and solving problems, because today's problems are complex. Working through them involves many different areas of expertise. Our interdisciplinary programs power you toward an academic degree and prepare you for the working world.
