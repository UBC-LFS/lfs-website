UBC is committed to preparing students to become exceptional global citizens. As described in the University's main Trek Vision Strategy, UBC strives to promote the values of a civil and sustainable society and to conduct outstanding research to serve the people of British Columbia, Canada and the world.

Global opportunities available through the Faculty of Land and Food Systems outlines our commitment to creating global leaders, capable of leading change in areas touching every aspect of our lives - land, food and water.
