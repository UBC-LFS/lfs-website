Student Services

The LFS Student Services team provides academic advising and support for all undergraduate students in a safe, welcoming and respectful environment. Through drop-in and booked advising appointments, we'll help you navigate your academic and non-academic journey at UBC and in LFS, answer your questions, and connect you with resources and support services across campus. From orientation to graduation, we work together with campus partners, your LFS Undergraduate Society (LFS|US) and other LFS student groups to put on events and programming to support your academic success â€” and to help you have some fun while you're here! When things feel a little intense, when you want to celebrate your successes, or even if you just want to grab a snack or work on the Student Services puzzle, we encourage you to come and see us.
