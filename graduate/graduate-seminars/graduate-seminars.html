Soil Science Seminar Series

This weekly seminar series features SOIL 500 students, and guests speaking on current and emerging topics in soil science. Topic areas include: soil chemistry, below ground ecology, biometeorology, land-water interactions, soil physics, and land-use impacts on soil quality.

TerreWEB Seminar Series

TerreWEB trains Master and PhD students from various backgrounds to become better communicators of their global change research, using TV, film, internet, mobile applications and more, in order to benefit the general public and contribute to a “greener future”. This innovative program includes researchers from UBC Faculties of Land and Food Systems, Forestry, Arts, Science, and Education, as well as Centre for Teaching, Learning and Technology (CTLT). The seminar series brings together current UBC students and invited guests to discuss issues and innovations around communicating global change science.
