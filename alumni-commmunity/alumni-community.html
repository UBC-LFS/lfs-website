ALUMNI & COMMUNITY

We are strengthened and enriched by our partnerships with the community beyond UBC's walls. In the Faculty of Land and Food Systems, these connections are of tremendous benefit to our students. As an alumnus or community member, we've got a ton of ways for you to contribute to our students' development as professionals — as well as your own — in the area of land and food systems.

As an alumnus, you're a member of UBC as well as our Land and Food Systems community.
