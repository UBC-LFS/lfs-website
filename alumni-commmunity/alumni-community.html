ALUMNI & COMMUNITY

The Faculty of Land and Food Systems fosters strong linkages and partnerships with the community through volunteerism, student initiatives and research. As a member of the community or alumni, there are numerous ways to stay connected with the Faculty including donations, volunteering, courses, presentations, community events, or hiring and mentoring students. If you are an alumni or community member interested in learning more about how you can get involved with the Faculty of Land and Food Systems, please visit our Alumni & Community Portal.
