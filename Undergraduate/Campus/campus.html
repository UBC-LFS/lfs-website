Build Your Experience: Campus

Volunteering in a student leader position or having a part-time job helps develop skills such as teamwork, public speaking, professional communication, people management, etc., that you otherwise might not be exposed to until much later on in your career. These campus opportunities can help you gain valuable experience while giving back and having fun!
