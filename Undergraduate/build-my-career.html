Build my career

There are many work and volunteer opportunities on campus.

LFS Academic & Career Engagement team

Contribute to the academic success and career development of students in the Faculty of Land and Food Systems, by helping students navigate academics, careers and personal wellbeing, through points of service or timely and relevant events that reach particular groups of students who need support and guidance.

Learn more

Community-Based Experiential Learning (CBEL)

Experiential learning is a core philosophy in our Faculty. Find out how you can connect with your community while gaining professional experience, working through complex community-based issues.

Learn more

Co-op

Jointly-run between the Faculties of Land and Food Systems and Science, this program offers students the opportunity to augment academic studies with full-time relevant paid work. Your experience will take the form of four four-month work placements at a variety of organizations.

Learn more

Tri-Mentoring Program

The Faculty's Tri-Mentoring program creates networking opportunities through "clusters" or "triads." Clusters/triads consist of a junior student (second or third year), a senior student (fourth year and above or graduate student) in the same program, and an industry-related professional.

Learn more

Work Learn

The Work Learn Program supports and subsidizes meaningful work experiences on campus that offer the opportunity for all current UBC students to develop your professional skills and learn in a work environment.

Learn more

UBC Careers Online

The UBC Careers Online website provides access to thousands of jobs targeted to UBC Students
